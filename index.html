Freeride ski is fun. Freeride ski is cool. - To learn freeride is not so easy. First when you go off pist you fail probable. There are a lot of things to master. But this is no problem but a opportunity. Step by step you get introduced to the art of Freeride.

iTechMe is a mobile application to learn Freeride ski by videos. The basic steps are shown in a serie of learn videos. To see the movement exactly you can zoom to details, show in slow motion, stop an repeat as much you like. The iTechMe Application also allows you to capture video yourself and then compare it with the provide best practices. You can also share your videos with friends and get comments and help from them.
